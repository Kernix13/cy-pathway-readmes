## Demo Day Student Guidelines & Tips

1. Introduction: 2-3 sentences about you - name, location, education, current job, background/experience, your goals, unique identifiers
2. Why did you choose Code:You? What do you hope to gain from the Code:You program?
3. Why did you choose this project?
4. What are the basic elements of your project?
5. What was the most challenging part of your project?
6. How did you overcome that challenge?
7. What did you learn about yourself and your skills? Or what problem does your project solve?
8. What is the most important thing you want employers to know about you?
9. End: contact information
